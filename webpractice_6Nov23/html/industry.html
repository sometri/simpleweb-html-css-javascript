Khmer University for Cambodian

Khmer University for Cambodian (KUC) is a renowned educational institution located in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. Founded in 2003, KUC is committed to providing high-quality education and shaping tomorrow's leaders. The university's motto is "In Pursuit of Knowledge and Wisdom, and Building Tomorrow's Leaders."

Academics

KUC offers a wide range of academic programs through its various colleges and schools. These include:

  • College of Arts and Humanities (CoAH)
  • College of Education (CoE)
  • College of Law (CoL)
  • College of Media and Communications (CMC)
  • College of Science and Technology (CoST)
  • College of Social Sciences (CoSS)
  • School of Creative Arts (SCA)
  • School of Foreign Languages (SFL)
  • Techo Sen School of Government and International Relations (TSS)
  • The Tony Fernandes School of Business (formerly the College of Management)
  • The School of Undergraduate Studies (SUS)
  • The School of Graduate Studies (SGS)

Scholarships

KUC offers scholarships to deserving students to support their educational journey. These scholarships help students access quality education and achieve their academic goals.
