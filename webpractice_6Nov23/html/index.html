Khmer University for Cambodian


Khmer University is a renowned educational institution located in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. It was established in 2003 with the aim of providing high-quality education and nurturing future leaders.

Academics

Khmer University offers a wide range of academic programs through its various colleges and schools. Some of the notable ones include:

  • College of Arts and Humanities (CoAH): Offers diverse majors in arts and humanities, catering to the students' and market needs.
  • College of Education (CoE): Focuses on providing comprehensive education programs for aspiring educators.
  • College of Law (CoL): Offers a rigorous curriculum to develop legal professionals equipped with practical skills and a deep understanding of the law.
  • College of Media and Communications (CMC): Provides students with knowledge and skills in the field of media and communications.
  • College of Science and Technology (CoST): Offers programs in science and technology, enabling students to excel in these fields.
  • College of Social Sciences (CoSS): Focuses on social sciences and equips students with a broader understanding of society.
  • School of Creative Arts (SCA): Provides programs in various creative arts disciplines, fostering artistic expression and creativity.
  • School of Foreign Languages (SFL): Offers language programs to enhance students' linguistic skills.
  • Techo Sen School of Government and International Relations (TSS): Focuses on training future leaders in the areas of government and international relations.
  • The Tony Fernandes School of Business (formerly the College of Management): Offers business programs to develop competent professionals in the field of business.
  • The School of Undergraduate Studies (SUS): Provides undergraduate programs across a range of disciplines.
  • The School of Graduate Studies (SGS): Offers various postgraduate programs for students to pursue advanced studies.

Facilities

Khmer University boasts state-of-the-art facilities to facilitate an enriching learning environment. Some of the notable facilities include:

  • The Handa Library: A well-equipped library that provides students with access to a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources.
  • Laboratories: Equipped with modern equipment to facilitate practical learning in various scientific disciplines.
  • Southeast Asia Television and Radio: A platform for students to gain practical experience in media production and broadcasting.

Scholarships

Khmer University offers scholarships to deserving students to support their education. These scholarships aim to encourage academic excellence and provide financial assistance to those in need.

Research

Khmer University promotes research and encourages faculty and students to engage in research activities. Through its research initiatives, the university aims to contribute to the development of knowledge and address emerging challenges in various fields.

Alumni

Khmer University takes pride in its accomplished alumni who have made significant contributions in their respective fields. Some notable alumni include individuals from academia, government, international institutions, media, non-profit organizations, and the private sector.

Partnership and Collaborations

Khmer University actively collaborates with local and international organizations to enhance its academic programs and research initiatives. These partnerships provide students with valuable opportunities for internships, scholarships, and exchange programs.
